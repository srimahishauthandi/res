About Us

Welcome to Zafran

Zafran is a celebration of heritage, flavor, and hospitality. Named after Saffron—the most precious spice in Indian cooking—our restaurant serves as an ambassador of authentic culinary arts. Every dish is cooked with love, precision, and the finest ingredients.

We harmonize traditional cooking methods like clay-pot slow cooking and intense clay-oven (tandoor) roasting with a modern presentation. Whether you dine in our warm, elegant dining hall or order to the comfort of your home, we promise an exceptional experience.
